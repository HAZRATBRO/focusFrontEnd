Admission Process

For Students of class 10th and 11th

A candidate seeking admission in any of the Classroom Program will have to write and qualify the Scholarship-cum-Admission Test called FTSE (Focus Talent Search Exam).

The scholarship and enrolment of student will be decided on the basis of performance in FTSE.

For Passout Students

For students in the Drop year , their admission purely depends on the JEE Main/ Advance scores.
